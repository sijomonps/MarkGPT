# MarkGPT Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-03-13

MarkGPT stores bookmark data locally in your browser using Chrome extension storage.

## Data We Collect
- Bookmark labels you enter
- Message snippets needed to locate bookmarked messages
- Chat URLs for navigation between chats
- Technical bookmark metadata (for matching and scrolling to the right message)

## How Data Is Used
- To save and display your bookmarks
- To navigate and scroll to bookmarked messages inside supported chat sites (ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini)

## Data Sharing
- MarkGPT does not sell data.
- MarkGPT does not send bookmark data to external servers.

## Data Retention and Deletion
- Data remains in your local browser storage until you remove bookmarks, clear extension data, or uninstall the extension.

## Permissions
- `storage`: Save bookmarks locally.
- Host access to `chat.openai.com`, `chatgpt.com`, `claude.ai`, and `gemini.google.com`: Inject content script only on supported chat pages.

## Contact
For support or privacy questions, provide your support email in the Chrome Web Store listing.
